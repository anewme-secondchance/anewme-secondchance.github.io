Family

Reunification Tips

  • Understand that things may feel different at first.
  • Allow children to express mixed emotions.
  • Be patient and consistent.
  • Create new healthy routines together.

Supervised Visit Tips

  • Arrive early and prepared.
  • Focus on positive interaction.
  • Avoid conflict topics.
  • Show responsibility and growth.

Rebuilding Trust

  • Keep promises.
  • Communicate openly.
  • Take responsibility for past actions.
  • Show consistency over time.

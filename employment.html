Employment

Interview Preparation

  • Wear clean, simple clothing.
  • Arrive 10–15 minutes early.
  • Practice answering common interview questions.
  • Be honest about your past but focus on growth.

Resume Building

  • Include prison work experience professionally.
  • List certifications and training completed.
  • Highlight teamwork, discipline, and reliability.

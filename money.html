Budget & Money

Weekly Budget Basics

  • Divide monthly rent by 4.
  • Set aside money for transportation first.
  • Plan for food ($50–$70 per week if possible).
  • Save even $5–$10 weekly.
  • Track all spending honestly.

How to Set Up a Bank Account

  • Bring State ID and Social Security Card.
  • Choose a bank that offers second-chance accounts.
  • Ask about monthly fees.
  • Set up direct deposit if possible.

Rebuilding Credit

  • Pay all bills on time.
  • Consider a secured credit card.
  • Keep balances low.
  • Avoid payday loans.
  • Check your credit report yearly.

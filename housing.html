Housing Stability

📄 Important Documents Needed

  • State ID or Driver’s License
  • Social Security Card
  • Birth Certificate
  • Proof of Income
  • Proof of Address

💡 Tips for Renting with a Record

  • Be honest but brief.
  • Provide references.
  • Show proof of stable income.
  • Offer larger deposit if needed.
  • Stay professional and confident.

Remember: Some landlords consider time since offense, rehabilitation, and income stability.
